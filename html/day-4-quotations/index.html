BEST AUTHOR AND THEIR QUOTS

Steve Jobs

1. "The only way to do great work is to love what you do."

2.If you haven't found it yet, keep looking. Don't settle. As with all matters of the heart, you'll know when you find it."

3. This quote emphasizes the importance of passion in achieving excellence. Steve Jobs believed that loving what you do is essential for producing outstanding work. It encourages individuals to pursue their true interests and not to settle for anything less than what they are passionate about, as this passion will drive them to excel and find fulfillment in their endeavors.
